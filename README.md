# 🚀 NEXUS360: Sales, Profitability & Supply Chain Intelligence

## 📊 Project Overview
A comprehensive Power BI analytics solution designed to evaluate sales performance, profitability trends, supplier risks, and supply chain efficiency across multiple regions.

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- 💰 Analyze Revenue & Profitability
- 📦 Monitor Supply Chain Performance
- ⚠️ Identify Supplier Risks
- 🌍 Evaluate Regional Performance
- 📈 Support Strategic Decision-Making

## 🔑 Key KPIs
- Revenue
- Profit
- Profit Margin
- Quantity Sold
- Supplier Quality Score
- Supply Chain Efficiency Score
- Fulfillment Lead Time
- Regional Profit Contribution

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel
- Figma
